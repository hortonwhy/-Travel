Kagawa Prefecture

Historical BackGround/Context

Kagawa Prefecture is located on the island of Shikoku. It is the smallest prefecture by geographic area. It also sits along the Seto Inland Sea which is connected to Honshu by the Great Seto Bridge, the world’s longest two-tiered bridge system. 11% of the total land area is designated as Natural Parks consisting of Setonaikai National Park and Otaki-Okawa Prefectural Natural Park, as well as miles of beautiful coastline. Kagawa Prefecture is a cultural hotspot for food as it is home to over 700 udon restaurants. It is also recognized for its famous dish hone-tsuki-dori and olive related products. (Source: Kagawa Official Site)
